Personify

A personalized music streaming app utilizing Spotify SDK.

Personify contains 4 features:

1) Discover New: Discover songs based on Spotify user's previously played tracks
2) Playlist Combination: Combines 4 different playlists into one big list
3) Search Track / Artist: Search & play any song or artist within the Spotify Database
4) History Played: Go back and listen to the last 30 songs you've listened to before

Finder

Apple Watch Restaurant Searcher using Yelp API

1. Finds nearby businesses (restaurant, store, shop, etc.) based on user's current location.
2. Sorts results based on options: Best Match, Closest Distance, or Ratings
3. Outputs directions to desired business

  • Tech Used: WatchKit, Yelp API, Core Location, MapKit, Alamofire, CocoaPods, SwiftyJSON
